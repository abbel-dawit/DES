About DES

A PLC built for Ethiopia's next decade

Digital Enterprise Solutions PLC was founded on a simple premise: institutions across Ethiopia deserve technology partners who understand the country's regulatory, linguistic, and infrastructural reality as well as they understand the code.

Headquartered in Addis Ababa, our engineers, trainers, and analysts work directly with public and private organizations across the country, not through intermediaries and not from abroad.

Through our global partnership with Infinite Icon Technology (Dubai, UAE), we bring international engineering standards and technology access to every local engagement.
